# Session Flow for DMs

*Official source: Chapter 1: **pp. 15-36***  
*Reference: [D&D 5E - Tomb of Annihilation.pdf](../../D&D%205E%20-%20Tomb%20of%20Annihilation.pdf)*

## Intro Scene

- Syndra explains the Death Curse and Soulmonger, hires the party, teleports them to Chult
  - *Syndra meeting scene: **pp. 7-8***

## Arrival at Port Nyanzaru

- Read/paraphrase harbor description (heat, color, dinosaurs, crowds) *(**pp. 15-16**)*
- Optional: Meet local official or Syndra's contact for guidance

## City Exploration and Social Play

- Players choose activities: taverns, temples, markets, arenas
- Use rumor tables and side quests to provide jungle information

## Side Quests

- Offer 1-2 low-level quests in/near city for XP and flavor
- Can introduce factions or guides for later relevance

## Outfitting and Choosing a Guide

- Handle shopping and logistics (gear, pack animals, canoes, insect repellent) *(special items: **p. 31**)*
- Present 3-4 guide options for players to choose *(finding guides: **pp. 33-35**)*

## Transition to Jungle

- Once equipped with guide, supplies, and destination, they leave the city *(Chapter 2 begins: **p. 37**)*
