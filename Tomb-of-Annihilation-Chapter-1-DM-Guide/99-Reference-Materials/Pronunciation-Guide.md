# Pronunciation Guide

*Reference: [D&D 5E - Tomb of Annihilation.pdf](../../D&D%205E%20-%20Tomb%20of%20Annihilation.pdf)*

Quick reference for pronouncing Chultan names, locations, and NPCs

**Note:** Pronunciations are based on official source material and phonetic guidelines. Some names may have multiple acceptable pronunciations.

## Quick Tips

1. **Chultan names** often have emphasis on the second or third syllable
2. **Apostrophes** in names (like O'tamu, N'baza, Pu'pu) typically indicate a glottal stop or slight pause
3. **'X' sounds** in Chultan names are often pronounced as 'Z' (e.g., Mwaxanare = mwah-ZAH-nah-reh)
4. **When in doubt**, emphasize the middle syllable of three-syllable names
5. **Consistency matters more than perfection** - pick a pronunciation and stick with it
