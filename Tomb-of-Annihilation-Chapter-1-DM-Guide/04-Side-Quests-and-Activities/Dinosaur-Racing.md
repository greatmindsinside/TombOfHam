# Dinosaur Racing

*Official source: Dinosaur racing: **pp. 31-33***  
*Reference: [D&D 5E - Tomb of Annihilation.pdf](../../D&D%205E%20-%20Tomb%20of%20Annihilation.pdf)*

## What to Say

> "The arena is packed with spectators, the air thick with excitement and the musky scent of dinosaurs. Brightly painted beasts snort and stamp, their riders adjusting saddles and exchanging last-minute tactics. The crowd roars as the starting signal sounds - a thunderous drumbeat that echoes through the stands.
>
> Your chosen dinosaur, a sleek deinonychus with emerald-green scales, explodes from the starting line. It weaves through the course, leaping over obstacles and outmaneuvering competitors. The crowd's cheers rise to a fever pitch as it crosses the finish line first!"

## What Players See

- A packed arena filled with excited spectators
- Colorful dinosaurs being prepared for races
- Professional jockeys and betting operations
- The vibrant, exotic culture of Port Nyanzaru
- Opportunities for entertainment, betting, and social connections

## Immediate Options for Players

1. **Watch the races** - Enjoy the spectacle and observe the scene
2. **Place bets** - Wager on race outcomes for potential gold
3. **Meet contacts** - Use the social setting to find underworld or faction contacts
4. **Investigate racing information** - Research odds and insider information
5. **Evaluate dinosaurs** - Use Nature checks to assess racing potential
6. **Participate in races** - If appropriate, enter as a jockey (optional)

## DM Guidance

Dinosaur racing is optional flavor content. Use it to show the exotic nature of Chult and provide social opportunities. Keep betting simple - use basic mechanics or just narrate exciting moments. Don't let it overshadow the main quest. Use the racing scene to introduce NPCs, provide information, or create complications. It's a good way to show the city's culture and give players a fun diversion.

## Skill Checks

### Perception/Investigation Checks

**DC 12 Perception Check**: Observe the racing scene and spot opportunities

- **Success**: "You notice details about the racing scene: which dinosaurs look strongest, which jockeys seem most skilled, and where the betting action is happening. You can identify potential opportunities for betting or social connections."
- **Failure**: "The arena is chaotic, and you can't get a clear read on the racing scene or spot specific opportunities."
- **Critical Success (17+)**: "Your observation is comprehensive: you can identify the strongest dinosaurs, the most skilled jockeys, potential betting opportunities, and even spot individuals who might be useful contacts (faction representatives, underworld figures, or other adventurers). You notice subtle details that could help you make better betting decisions or find valuable connections."

**DC 13 Investigation Check**: Research racing information and betting odds

- **Success**: "By asking around and investigating, you can learn about the racing scene: which dinosaurs are favored, which jockeys have good records, and what the betting odds are. You can make more informed betting decisions."
- **Failure**: "The racing information is difficult to gather, and you can't determine reliable betting information."
- **Critical Success (18+)**: "Your investigation reveals detailed information: you learn about each dinosaur's racing history, each jockey's track record, insider information about potential race outcomes, and even rumors about fixed races or opportunities. You can identify the best betting opportunities and might even discover ways to gain an advantage."

### Additional Skill Checks

**DC 11 Insight Check**: Read the crowd and detect scams or fixed races

- **Success**: "You can sense when something is off about the racing scene. Some races might be fixed, some bookmakers might be running scams, and some individuals might be trying to take advantage of newcomers. You can identify which opportunities are legitimate and which to avoid."
- **Failure**: "The racing scene seems legitimate, but you can't be certain if there are scams or fixed races happening."
- **Critical Success (16+)**: "Your insight is sharp: you can detect specific scams, identify fixed races, spot dishonest bookmakers, and even sense when someone is trying to manipulate you. You can avoid dangerous situations and identify the most trustworthy opportunities."

**DC 12 Nature Check**: Evaluate dinosaur capabilities and racing potential

- **Success**: "Your knowledge of animals helps you evaluate the racing dinosaurs. You can identify which dinosaurs are best suited for racing, which are in peak condition, and which might have advantages or disadvantages. This helps you make better betting decisions."
- **Failure**: "The dinosaurs all seem capable, but you can't determine which ones are most likely to win."
- **Critical Success (17+)**: "Your nature knowledge is extensive: you can identify specific dinosaur species, evaluate their physical condition, assess their racing capabilities, and even predict how they'll perform based on their breed, training, and condition. You can make highly informed betting decisions and might even spot opportunities others miss."

**DC 11 Persuasion Check**: Negotiate better betting odds or gather information

- **Success**: "Your diplomatic approach helps you negotiate better betting odds, get insider information, or build relationships with bookmakers, jockeys, or other racing enthusiasts. You might gain access to better opportunities or valuable connections."
- **Failure**: "The bookmakers stick to their standard odds and aren't willing to negotiate or share information."
- **Critical Success (16+)**: "Your negotiation is highly effective: you can secure significantly better betting odds, gain insider information about upcoming races, and even build relationships with key figures in the racing scene. You might gain access to exclusive betting opportunities or valuable contacts."

**DC 10 Sleight of Hand Check**: Detect cheating or attempt to cheat (if applicable)

- **Success**: "You can detect if someone is cheating in the races or if bookmakers are running scams. You can identify dishonest practices and avoid being taken advantage of."
- **Failure**: "The racing seems fair, but you can't be certain if there's cheating happening."
- **Critical Success (15+)**: "Your ability to detect cheating is sharp: you can identify specific cheating methods, spot dishonest bookmakers, and even catch subtle scams. You can avoid dangerous situations and might even expose cheaters, potentially gaining rewards or favors."

## NPC Stats

### Race Organizer

- **Role**: Manages racing events and arena operations
- **Personality**: Business-focused, organized, profit-driven
- **Knowledge**:
  - Information about racing schedules and events
  - Knowledge of dinosaurs and jockeys
  - Understanding of betting operations
  - Connections to various racing-related NPCs
- **Interaction DCs**:
  - **Persuasion (DC 12)**: Get information or better access
  - **Insight (DC 11)**: Detect if races are fixed or manipulated
  - **Investigation (DC 12)**: Learn about racing operations

### Professional Jockey

- **Role**: Rides dinosaurs in races
- **Personality**: Competitive, skilled, confident, potentially arrogant
- **Knowledge**:
  - Extensive knowledge of racing techniques
  - Understanding of dinosaur behavior and capabilities
  - Information about other jockeys and their methods
  - Knowledge of racing strategies
- **Interaction DCs**:
  - **Persuasion (DC 13)**: Get insider information or tips
  - **Nature (DC 12)**: Discuss dinosaurs to gain their respect
  - **Insight (DC 11)**: Detect if they're being honest about race outcomes

### Bookmaker

- **Role**: Manages betting operations
- **Personality**: Calculating, profit-focused, potentially dishonest
- **Knowledge**:
  - Information about betting odds and payouts
  - Knowledge of racing statistics and outcomes
  - Understanding of betting operations
  - May have insider information about races
- **Interaction DCs**:
  - **Persuasion (DC 12)**: Negotiate better odds
  - **Insight (DC 12)**: Detect scams or dishonest practices
  - **Deception (DC 11)**: Detect if they're running scams
- **Secrets/Agendas**:
  - May run scams or fixed betting operations
  - Profit-focused and may take advantage of newcomers
  - Could be a source of information if handled carefully

## Racing Basics

- **Venue**: Outdoor arena near the harbor
- **Animals**: Various dinosaurs (deinonychus, ankylosaurus, etc.)
- **Riders**: Professional jockeys or amateurs
- **Betting**: Legal and widespread

## How to Run a Race

1. **Describe the Scene**: Crowded arena, cheering crowds, colorful dinosaurs
2. **Present Betting Options**: Let players wager on races
3. **Run the Race**: Use simple mechanics or just narrate exciting moments
4. **Resolve Bets**: Award winnings or losses

## Purpose in Chapter 1

- **Tone Setting**: Shows the colorful, exotic nature of Chult
- **Social Hook**: Players can meet underworld contacts or faction representatives
- **XP/Flavor**: Light combat encounters or skill challenges around races
- **Optional**: Not required, but fun for players who enjoy gambling or dinosaurs
