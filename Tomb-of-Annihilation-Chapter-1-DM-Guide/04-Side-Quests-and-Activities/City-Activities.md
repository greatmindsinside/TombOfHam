# City Activities Overview

*Official source: Things to Do header: **p. 31***  
*Reference: [D&D 5E - Tomb of Annihilation.pdf](../../D&D%205E%20-%20Tomb%20of%20Annihilation.pdf)*

## What to Say

> "Port Nyanzaru is a city that never sleeps, and there's always something happening. As you explore, you'll find countless opportunities to engage with the city's vibrant culture, gather information, and prepare for your journey into the jungle. Whether you're seeking rumors in a crowded tavern, shopping for supplies in the bustling markets, seeking blessings at the temples, or simply watching the spectacle of dinosaur racing, every activity is a chance to learn more about Chult and its people.
>
> The city rewards those who take the time to explore and engage. Every conversation might reveal a clue, every purchase might provide a crucial tool, and every encounter might lead to a valuable connection. But be careful - not everyone in Port Nyanzaru is friendly, and some activities might lead you into danger even within the city walls."

## What Players Discover

- Social activities: taverns, markets, temples, arena spectacles
- Adventure activities: side quests, street encounters, faction meetings
- Preparation activities: guide research, supply gathering, information gathering
- Various locations throughout the city offering different opportunities
- The vibrant, exotic culture of Port Nyanzaru

## Immediate Options for Players

1. **Visit taverns** - Gather rumors, meet contacts, socialize
2. **Shop in markets** - Buy supplies, overhear conversations
3. **Attend temples** - Get blessings, learn about local gods
4. **Watch spectacles** - Enjoy dinosaur racing or other entertainment
5. **Seek side quests** - Find missions for XP and connections
6. **Research guides** - Learn about potential jungle guides
7. **Gather supplies** - Stock up on expedition gear
8. **Meet factions** - Contact organization representatives

## DM Guidance

Let players drive the action - what do they want to do? Use activities to build anticipation for the jungle while showing the city's culture. Balance opportunities with potential dangers. Each activity should provide value (information, connections, resources). Don't overwhelm players with too many options at once. Use activities to introduce complications that show jungle dangers exist even in the city. Keep energy high with colorful, exotic descriptions.

## Skill Checks

### Perception/Investigation Checks

**DC 12 Perception Check**: Notice activity opportunities and interesting locations

- **Success**: "You notice various activity opportunities: interesting taverns, bustling markets, impressive temples, and exciting spectacles. You can identify which activities might be most valuable for gathering information or preparing for your journey."
- **Failure**: "The city is busy and overwhelming, and you can't identify specific activity opportunities."
- **Critical Success (17+)**: "Your observation is comprehensive: you can identify multiple valuable activity opportunities, spot connections between different activities, notice which locations are most likely to provide useful information, and even detect potential dangers or scams. You can prioritize which activities to pursue."

**DC 13 Investigation Check**: Research activities and gather information about them

- **Success**: "By asking around and investigating, you can learn about various activities: which taverns have the best rumors, which markets have the best prices, which temples offer useful blessings, and which activities might lead to valuable connections or information."
- **Failure**: "The activity information is difficult to gather, and you can't determine which activities are most valuable."
- **Critical Success (18+)**: "Your investigation reveals detailed information: you learn about the best locations for each activity type, insider information about prices and opportunities, connections to valuable NPCs, and even hidden activities or locations that others might miss. You can make highly informed decisions about which activities to pursue."

### Additional Skill Checks

**DC 11 Insight Check**: Read social situations and detect opportunities or dangers

- **Success**: "You can sense which activities are safe and which might be dangerous. You can identify opportunities for valuable connections, detect potential scams or threats, and understand the social dynamics of different activities."
- **Failure**: "The activities seem safe enough, but you can't be certain if there are hidden dangers or opportunities."
- **Critical Success (16+)**: "Your insight is sharp: you can detect specific opportunities or dangers in each activity, identify which NPCs are trustworthy, sense hidden agendas or scams, and even predict how different activities might lead to valuable connections or information. You can avoid dangerous situations and maximize your opportunities."

**DC 12 Persuasion Check**: Build relationships through activities

- **Success**: "Your friendly approach helps you build relationships through activities. People are more willing to share information, offer discounts, or provide assistance. You might gain valuable contacts or allies."
- **Failure**: "People remain polite but distant, and you don't build meaningful relationships through activities."
- **Critical Success (17+)**: "Your social skills are highly effective: you can build strong relationships through activities, gain valuable contacts and allies, secure better deals or information, and even convince people to provide assistance or resources. You might gain ongoing relationships that help you throughout your journey."

**DC 10 History Check**: Recall information about activities or locations

- **Success**: "You remember hearing about some of these activities or locations before. Their reputations or histories ring a bell, and you can recall basic information that helps you navigate them more effectively."
- **Failure**: "The activities and locations are unfamiliar, and you don't have prior knowledge about them."
- **Critical Success (15+)**: "Your historical knowledge is extensive: you recall detailed information about activities, locations, and their histories. You know about their reputations, past events, and even connections to other locations or NPCs. This knowledge helps you navigate activities more effectively and might reveal hidden opportunities."

## Things to Do in Port Nyanzaru

The city offers various activities for players to engage with:

### Social Activities

#### Tavern Hopping

- **Purpose**: Gather rumors, meet contacts, build relationships
- **Skill Checks**: Persuasion (DC 12), Insight (DC 11), Investigation (DC 12)
- **Potential Rewards**: Information, contacts, rumors, potential quest hooks

#### Market Shopping

- **Purpose**: Buy supplies, overhear conversations, gather information
- **Skill Checks**: Investigation (DC 12), Persuasion (DC 12), Insight (DC 11)
- **Potential Rewards**: Supplies, information, contacts, better prices

#### Temple Visits

- **Purpose**: Get blessings, learn about local gods, gather information
- **Skill Checks**: Religion (DC 12), Persuasion (DC 11), Insight (DC 12)
- **Potential Rewards**: Blessings, information, potential quest hooks, religious connections

#### Arena Watching

- **Purpose**: Dinosaur races and other spectacles, social connections
- **Skill Checks**: Perception (DC 12), Insight (DC 11), Persuasion (DC 12)
- **Potential Rewards**: Entertainment, social connections, potential betting opportunities

### Adventure Activities

#### Side Quests

- **Purpose**: Low-level missions for XP and connections
- **Skill Checks**: Varies by quest (see Side-Quests.md)
- **Potential Rewards**: XP, gold, information, faction favor, equipment

#### Street Encounters

- **Purpose**: Random events that provide flavor and opportunities
- **Skill Checks**: Varies by encounter
- **Potential Rewards**: Information, connections, potential quest hooks, complications

#### Faction Meetings

- **Purpose**: Seek aid or information from organizations
- **Skill Checks**: Persuasion (DC 13), Insight (DC 12), History (DC 12)
- **Potential Rewards**: Information, resources, faction favor, potential allies

### Preparation Activities

#### Guide Research

- **Purpose**: Learn about potential jungle guides
- **Skill Checks**: Investigation (DC 13), Insight (DC 12), History (DC 11)
- **Potential Rewards**: Information about guides, better guide selection

#### Supply Gathering

- **Purpose**: Stock up on gear for expedition
- **Skill Checks**: Investigation (DC 12), Persuasion (DC 12), Insight (DC 11)
- **Potential Rewards**: Supplies, better prices, information about needed items

#### Information Gathering

- **Purpose**: Research jungle locations and dangers
- **Skill Checks**: Investigation (DC 13), History (DC 12), Persuasion (DC 12)
- **Potential Rewards**: Information about jungle, locations, dangers, potential routes

## Sample Street Encounter

### What to Say (Street Encounter)

> "As you navigate the crowded streets, you hear a commotion ahead. A panicked merchant shouts, 'Look out! The beast is loose!' Suddenly, a juvenile triceratops bursts through a fruit stand, scattering wares everywhere. The crowd scatters as the confused dinosaur charges down the street."

### Skill Checks (Street Encounter)

- **Perception (DC 12)**: Notice the dinosaur's approach and react in time
- **Animal Handling (DC 13)**: Calm or redirect the dinosaur
- **Nature (DC 12)**: Understand the dinosaur's behavior and how to handle it
