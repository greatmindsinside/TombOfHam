- **Official Source**: [D&D 5E - Tomb of Annihilation.pdf](../D&D%205E%20-%20Tomb%20of%20Annihilation.pdf) (located in project root)

---

This folder structure is organized chronologically to help Dungeon Masters run Chapter 1 of Tomb of Annihilation in sequence. Each numbered folder represents a phase of the session that you should follow in order.

## Folder Structure

### 00-Session-Prep

Pre-session preparation materials, overview, and setup notes.

### 01-Introduction-and-Hook

The opening scene with Syndra Silvane and the Death Curse explanation.

### 02-Arrival-in-Port-Nyanzaru

Party arrival in the city, first impressions, and harbor description.

### 03-City-Exploration-and-Social-Play

Social encounters, meeting NPCs, gathering rumors, and exploring locations.

### 04-Side-Quests-and-Activities

Optional side quests, dinosaur racing, and city activities.

### 05-Outfitting-and-Preparation

Shopping, hiring guides, gathering supplies, and expedition preparation.

### 06-Chapter-Transition

Leaving the city and transitioning to Chapter 2: The Land of Chult.

### 99-Reference-Materials

Additional reference materials, page numbers, and alternative structures.
