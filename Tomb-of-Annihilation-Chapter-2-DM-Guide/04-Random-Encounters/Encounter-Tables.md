# Random Encounter Tables

*Official source: Random encounters: **pp. 40-41***  
*Reference: [D&D 5E - Tomb of Annihilation.pdf](../../D&D%205E%20-%20Tomb%20of%20Annihilation.pdf)*

**Location:** Jungle (Various Hexes)

## Overview

Players face 1-2 random encounters per day of travel. Use these tables to determine what they encounter, or choose encounters that advance the plot or provide information.

## Daily Encounter Roll

**Roll 1d12 (or choose based on story needs):**

1. **Dinosaur Herd** (herbivores) → [Dinosaur-Encounters.md](./Dinosaur-Encounters.md)
2. **Predator Attack** (carnivorous dinosaur) → [Dinosaur-Encounters.md](./Dinosaur-Encounters.md)
3. **Undead** (zombies/skeletons) → [Undead-Encounters.md](./Undead-Encounters.md)
4. **Weather Event** (storm, heat wave) → [Weather-Mechanics.md](../05-Weather-and-Survival/Weather-Mechanics.md)
5. **Disease Vector** (mosquitoes, contaminated water) → [Disease-Mechanics.md](../05-Weather-and-Survival/Disease-Mechanics.md)
6. **Discovery** (ruins, trail, camp) → Exploration opportunity
7. **Tribal Encounter** (Batiri goblins, Chultan natives) → [Tribal-Encounters.md](./Tribal-Encounters.md)
8. **Natural Hazard** (quicksand, falling tree, flash flood) → [Natural-Hazards.md](./Natural-Hazards.md)
9. **Other Adventurers** (friendly or hostile) → Social encounter
10. **Magical Anomaly** (strange magic, portal, artifact) → Mystery/opportunity
11. **Animal Attack** (giant snakes, spiders, etc.) → Combat encounter
12. **No Encounter** - Peaceful travel day

## Encounter Frequency

### Normal Travel

- **1-2 encounters per day** (DM choice)
- Mix combat, exploration, and social encounters
- Use encounters to advance plot when possible

### Dangerous Areas

- **2-3 encounters per day**
- Higher chance of combat encounters
- More dangerous creatures and hazards

### Safe Routes

- **0-1 encounters per day**
- More discovery and social encounters
- Lower combat risk

## Using Encounters

### Plot Advancement

- Use encounters to provide information about Omu
- Connect encounters to the Soulmonger's influence
- Introduce NPCs or locations that advance the story

### Survival Challenge

- Use encounters to drain resources
- Create situations requiring resource management
- Test players' preparation and supplies

### World Building

- Show the diversity of Chult's dangers
- Introduce different types of threats
- Build atmosphere and tension
