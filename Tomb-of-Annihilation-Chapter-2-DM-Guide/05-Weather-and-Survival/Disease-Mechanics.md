# Disease Mechanics

*Official source: Disease: **pp. 42-43***  
*Reference: [D&D 5E - Tomb of Annihilation.pdf](../../D&D%205E%20-%20Tomb%20of%20Annihilation.pdf)*

**Location:** Jungle (Daily Disease Checks)

## Overview

Disease is a constant threat in the jungle. Mosquitoes, contaminated water, and infected wounds can all cause diseases that weaken and potentially kill players.

## Disease Sources

### Mosquitoes

- **Exposure**: Daily when not using insect repellent
- **Save**: DC 10 Constitution save
- **Disease**: Shivering sickness, malaria
- **Prevention**: Insect repellent (1 gp standard, 5 gp premium paste)

### Contaminated Water

- **Exposure**: Drinking from unsafe water sources
- **Save**: DC 10 Constitution save
- **Disease**: Dysentery, waterborne illnesses
- **Prevention**: Purify water, use safe sources

### Infected Wounds

- **Exposure**: Injuries that aren't properly treated
- **Save**: DC 12 Constitution save
- **Disease**: Infection, sepsis
- **Prevention**: Proper medical treatment

## Disease Progression

### Level 1: Initial Infection

- **Effects**: Disadvantage on ability checks
- **Cure**: Medicine check (DC 15) or lesser restoration

### Level 2: Worsening Condition

- **Effects**: Disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws
- **Cure**: Medicine check (DC 15) or lesser restoration

### Level 3+: Advanced Disease

- **Effects**: Hit point maximum reduced, risk of death
- **Cure**: Greater restoration or powerful magic
