# Session Flow for Chapter 2

*Official source: Chapter 2: **pp. 37+***  
*Reference: [D&D 5E - Tomb of Annihilation.pdf](../../D&D%205E%20-%20Tomb%20of%20Annihilation.pdf)*

## First Day in the Jungle

- Players leave Port Nyanzaru and enter the wilderness
- First impressions of jungle travel
- Initial encounter to establish danger
- Guide takes the lead in navigation

## Daily Travel Routine

**Morning:**

- Guide makes navigation check
- Weather roll
- Disease check (if applicable)
- Decide travel pace

**Travel:**

- Describe terrain and environment
- Random encounter (1-2 per day)
- Resource consumption (water, food)
- Navigation challenges

**Evening:**

- Find camp site
- Set watch
- Rest (if safe)
- Plan next day's route

## Key Location Visits

- Players choose destinations or encounter locations
- Location interactions: NPCs, information, resources
- Decision to stay, explore, or move on
- Information gathering about Omu

## Information Gathering

- Multiple sources reveal Omu's location
- Oracle at Orolunga (most direct)
- NPCs at various locations
- Clues in ruins and ancient sites
- Following undead activity

## Transition to Chapter 3

- Players learn Omu is the destination
- Final journey to the lost city
- Prepare for Chapter 3: The Lost City of Omu
