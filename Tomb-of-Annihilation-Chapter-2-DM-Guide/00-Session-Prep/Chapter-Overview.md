# Chapter 2 Overview: The Land of Chult - Wilderness Exploration

*Official source: Chapter 2 begins on **p. 37***  
*Reference: [D&D 5E - Tomb of Annihilation.pdf](../../D&D%205E%20-%20Tomb%20of%20Annihilation.pdf)*

## Purpose of Chapter 2

Chapter 2 of Tomb of Annihilation serves as "The Land of Chult: Wilderness Exploration." The chapter focuses on hex-crawl wilderness travel through the dangerous jungles of Chult, where players must navigate, survive, and discover the location of the Soulmonger. It's designed to create a sense of danger, discovery, and survival challenge as players explore the untamed wilderness.

## Chapter Structure

The book uses Chapter 2 for several main purposes:

### 1. Wilderness Travel and Survival

- Hex-crawl mechanics for navigation and exploration
- Resource management: food, water, supplies
- Survival challenges: disease, weather, exhaustion
- The constant threat of getting lost or running out of supplies

### 2. Key Locations

- **Fort Beluarian**: Flaming Fist stronghold for resupply and information
- **Camp Vengeance**: Order of the Gauntlet camp under siege
- **Orolunga**: Oracle ziggurat that can reveal Omu's location
- **Kir Sabal**: Aarakocra village offering unique perspectives
- **Mbala**: Hag's lair with dangerous information
- **Nangalore**: Ancient ruins with deadly guardians
- And many other locations throughout the jungle

### 3. Random Encounters

- Dinosaur encounters (herbivores and predators)
- Undead encounters (zombies, skeletons, suggesting Soulmonger's influence)
- Tribal encounters (Batiri goblins, Chultan natives)
- Natural hazards (storms, quicksand, flash floods)
- Other adventurers and explorers

### 4. Information Gathering

- Learning about the Soulmonger's location (Omu)
- Gathering clues from NPCs, ruins, and locations
- Following leads and rumors
- Discovering the path to the lost city

### 5. Survival Mechanics

- Weather system (storms, extreme heat)
- Disease mechanics (mosquitoes, contaminated water)
- Exhaustion from heat, forced march, or disease
- Finding food, water, and safe shelter

## Chapter Goals

- Players learn the Soulmonger is in Omu (the lost city)
- Players experience the dangers of jungle travel
- Players visit key locations that advance the plot
- Players gather information and resources
- Players prepare for the journey to Omu

**Transition**: Move to Chapter 3 when players learn Omu's location and begin the final approach to the lost city.
