## Session Overview

- **Expected Duration:** 3-4 hours

- **Main NPCs:** (See Camp Vengeance location file for NPCs)

## Opening (5 min)

**What to Say:**

> "After days of travel through the jungle, you spot a fortified camp ahead. Paladins and clerics in the colors of the Order of the Gauntlet patrol the walls, but even from a distance, you can see the camp is under siege. Undead creatures press against the defenses, and the defenders look exhausted."

**If Players Ask About:** Link to [Camp Vengeance Details](../03-Key-Locations/Camp-Vengeance.md)

---

## Scene 1: Approaching the Camp (15 min)

**Setup:**

- Location: Jungle approach to Camp Vengeance

- Mood: Desperate, dangerous, heroic

**What to Say:**

> "As you draw closer, the full extent of the camp's troubles becomes clear. Zombies and skeletons press against the palisade walls, and you can see defenders fighting on the battlements. The camp's banner—a gauntleted fist—flies above, but it's tattered and bloodstained."

**Key Information:**

- Camp is under constant undead assault

- Order of the Gauntlet is struggling to hold

- Commander Breakbone needs help

**Player Options:**

- Approach and offer aid

- Observe from a distance

- Try to sneak around the camp

- Attack the undead from behind

---

## Scene 2: Meeting Commander Breakbone (20 min)

**Setup:**

- Location: Camp Vengeance command tent

- NPC: (See [Camp Vengeance](../03-Key-Locations/Camp-Vengeance.md) for NPC details)

- Mood: Desperate but determined

**What to Say:**

> "Commander Breakbone is a haggard paladin, her armor dented and her face lined with exhaustion. She looks up from a map as you enter. 'Thank the gods you've come. We've been under siege for weeks. The undead keep coming, drawn by something in the jungle interior. We need help—supplies, reinforcements, or someone to find the source of these attacks.'"

**Key Information to Convey:**

- Undead are being drawn from the jungle interior

- Camp is running low on supplies

- They need help to survive

- They know something powerful is in the jungle

**Possible Player Questions:**

- "What's causing the undead?" → Points toward Soulmonger (future chapter)

- "How can we help?" → See [Camp Vengeance](../03-Key-Locations/Camp-Vengeance.md) for quest details

- "What do you know about the Death Curse?" → They've heard rumors but don't know details

**Skill Checks:**

- Insight DC 12: She's desperate but trustworthy

- History DC 11: Recall Order of the Gauntlet's reputation

- Persuasion DC 13: Negotiate aid or information exchange

---

## Combat Encounter: Undead Assault (45 min)

**Trigger:** If players agree to help defend the camp

**Setup:**

- 8 zombies (use [Zombie stat block](./monster-stats.md#zombie) | [Undead Encounters](../04-Random-Encounters/Undead-Encounters.md))

- 4 skeletons (use [Skeleton stat block](./monster-stats.md#skeleton) | [Undead Encounters](../04-Random-Encounters/Undead-Encounters.md))

- 1 ghoul (use [Ghoul stat block](./monster-stats.md#ghoul) | [Undead Encounters](../04-Random-Encounters/Undead-Encounters.md))

- Map: Camp perimeter with palisade walls

**Tactics:**

- Undead attack in waves

- Players can fight from walls or outside

- Commander Breakbone fights alongside party

- Undead retreat if leader (ghoul) is killed

**Loot:**

- 50 gp from fallen undead

- Information about undead source direction

- Gratitude from Order of the Gauntlet

---

## Closing (5 min)

**What to Say:**

> "With the immediate threat repelled, Commander Breakbone thanks you. 'You've given us a reprieve, but the attacks will continue until someone finds the source. The undead are being drawn from the jungle interior—something powerful is calling them. If you're heading that way, be careful. And if you find what's causing this... destroy it.'"

**End-of-Session Checklist:**

- [ ] Players helped defend the camp?

- [ ] Players learned about undead source?

- [ ] Players received information about jungle locations?

- [ ] Award XP for combat and roleplay
