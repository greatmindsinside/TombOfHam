---

This folder structure is organized chronologically to help Dungeon Masters run Chapter 2 of Tomb of Annihilation in sequence. Each numbered folder represents a phase of the session that you should follow in order.

## Folder Structure

### 00-Session-Prep

Pre-session preparation materials, overview, and setup notes for wilderness exploration.

### 01-Entering-the-Jungle

The first day in the jungle, initial encounters, and first impressions of wilderness travel.

### 02-Travel-Mechanics

Hex crawling, navigation, resource management, and getting lost in the jungle.

### 03-Key-Locations

Major destinations in the jungle: Fort Beluarian, Camp Vengeance, Orolunga, Kir Sabal, Mbala, Nangalore, and others.

### 04-Random-Encounters

Encounter tables, dinosaur encounters, undead encounters, tribal encounters, and natural hazards.

### 05-Weather-and-Survival

Weather mechanics, disease rules, exhaustion management, and survival challenges.

### 06-Information-Gathering

How players learn about Omu's location, information from NPCs, and gathering clues.

### 07-Chapter-Transition

Requirements for transitioning to Chapter 3 (Omu) and the journey to the lost city.

### 99-Reference-Materials

Additional reference materials, maps, quick reference tables, and alternative structures.
