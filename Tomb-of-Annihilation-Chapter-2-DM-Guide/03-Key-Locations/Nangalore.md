# Nangalore: Ancient Ruins

*Official source: Nangalore: **pp. [page numbers]***  
*Reference: [D&D 5E - Tomb of Annihilation.pdf](../../D&D%205E%20-%20Tomb%20of%20Annihilation.pdf)*

**Location:** Nangalore, Ancient Garden Ruins

## Overview

Nangalore is the beautiful but deadly ruins of an ancient garden. The place is overrun with dangerous creatures but may contain valuable information or treasure.

## What to Say

> "Through the jungle, you catch glimpses of something extraordinary - the remains of an ancient garden, still beautiful despite centuries of neglect. Stone pathways wind through overgrown vegetation, and you can see the remains of statues, fountains, and structures that once must have been magnificent.
>
> Your guide pauses. 'Nangalore. Ancient garden ruins. Beautiful, but deadly. The place is overrun with dangerous creatures - medusas, yuan-ti, things that turn you to stone or worse. But ancient places like this often hold secrets - information about Chult's history, or clues about the Soulmonger. It's dangerous, but potentially valuable.'"

## What Players Find

- Ancient garden ruins
- Dangerous creatures (medusa, yuan-ti, etc.)
- Potential treasure and magic items
- Clues about Chult's history
- Information about the Soulmonger or Omu
- Combat and exploration challenges

## Immediate Options for Players

1. **Explore the ruins** - Enter and investigate
2. **Assess the danger** - Use skill checks to identify threats
3. **Combat encounters** - Face the creatures within
4. **Search for clues** - Look for information about Omu or the Soulmonger
5. **Avoid the ruins** - Continue travel without exploring
6. **Plan approach** - Strategize before entering
