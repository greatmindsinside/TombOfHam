**Location:** Fort Beluarian, Flaming Fist Stronghold

## Overview

Fort Beluarian is a Flaming Fist military outpost on the edge of civilization. It serves as a resupply point, information source, and safe haven for expeditions.

## What to Say

> "Through the jungle, you spot a fortified compound - Fort Beluarian. High walls, watchtowers, and the distinctive red and gold banners of the Flaming Fist mark this as a military outpost. The fort is well-maintained and clearly designed to withstand jungle threats.
>
> Your guide nods. 'Fort Beluarian. Flaming Fist stronghold. They maintain order in this part of the jungle. If you have a charter, they'll be friendly. If not... well, they might be suspicious. But they have supplies, information, and it's one of the safest places in the jungle.'"

## What Players Find

- Flaming Fist soldiers and officers
- Supplies for purchase (food, water, equipment)
- Information about jungle dangers and locations
- Safe place to rest and recover
- Potential quests or missions
- Maps and reports about undead activity

## Immediate Options for Players

1. **Enter the fort** - Approach and request entry
2. **Show charter** - Present Flaming Fist expedition charter (if obtained)
3. **Seek information** - Ask about jungle locations, undead activity, or the Soulmonger
4. **Resupply** - Purchase food, water, and equipment
5. **Rest safely** - Take a long rest in safety
6. **Meet commander** - Speak with Fort Beluarian's leader
